06. Monthly Review

Reviewing as we go.

After the first month, we'll meet to review what's working, refine the process and discuss whether we'd like to expand the scope of the work.

Potential areas for future growth include:

  • Reviewing content performance and audience engagement.
  • Refining the content strategy and posting schedule.
  • Expanding to additional platforms (e.g. Facebook, TikTok or LinkedIn).
  • Community management and customer engagement (responding to comments and messages).
  • Expanding content creation, including on site filming where appropriate.
  • Exploring paid advertising opportunities.
  • Developing new content series and campaigns.
  • Increasing posting frequency where appropriate.
  • Identifying new opportunities to support business growth.

The monthly review ensures the strategy can evolve alongside the business while keeping the workload, investment and priorities aligned with Unisus' goals.
